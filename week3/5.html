Dance Styles

Hip Hop

Hip hop comes from the streets — raw, real, and full of energy. With strong grooves, musicality, and attitude, this style lets dancers tell their story through movement.

Waacking

It's a high-energy dance style known for its sharp arm movements, strong poses, and expressive attitude. Born in the disco era, it’s all about confidence, rhythm, and self-expression.

House

House dance comes from the clubs, driven by the beat and guided by the soul. With fast footwork, fluid movement, and nonstop groove, house is freedom in motion.
